Programme Leadership
Delivering the Undeliverable
What separates programmes that succeed from those that quietly collapse? Ivan shares 20 years of hard-won lessons about governance, stakeholder management, and the courage to make decisions when the data isn't perfect. A candid, experience-driven talk that challenges conventional PM wisdom.
02
PM Excellence
The PMP Mindset: Beyond the Certification
PMP is the beginning, not the destination. Ivan explores what it really means to operate at the highest levels of project professionalism — the habits, judgment, and ethical grounding that no exam can teach. Ideal for PMI chapter events and professional development conferences.
03
Future of PM
AI & the Project Manager of 2030
Artificial intelligence is reshaping how projects are planned, monitored, and delivered. Ivan cuts through the hype to explore what will actually change — and what won't. Practical, grounded, and forward-looking. A session that audiences consistently rate as a highlight.

Leadership
Leading Teams Across Cultures
International delivery is harder than international travel. Drawing on experience across Europe, the Americas, and Africa, Ivan shares a framework for building trust, resolving conflict, and creating cohesion in teams that were never designed to work together.
